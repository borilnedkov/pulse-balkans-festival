Pulse of the Balkans Eco Music Festival

Get ready for an unforgettable weekend where music meets nature! Pulse of the Balkans is a unique open-air festival blending the hottest beats of Bulgarian pop music and the rich sounds of Bulgarian pop-folk (chalga)—all with a modern, eco-conscious twist.

Set against the stunning natural backdrop of Veliko Tarnovo, immerse yourself in 3 days of vibrant live performances, artistic expression, and green living.

Dance under the stars, celebrate Balkan culture, and be part of a sustainable future.
